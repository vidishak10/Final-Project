Chronic Lyme Disease Information

Definition:

Chronic Lyme disease, also known as post-treatment Lyme disease syndrome (PTLDS), is a condition characterized by lingering symptoms that persist despite receiving appropriate treatment for Lyme disease.

Common Symptoms:

  • Fatigue
  • Muscle aches
  • Joint pain
  • Cognitive difficulties (brain fog)
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Headaches
  • Neck stiffness
  • Depression
  • Anxiety

Chronic Lyme disease can significantly impact a person's quality of life and may require a multidisciplinary approach to management, including medications, lifestyle modifications, and supportive therapies.
